Marianne Allure

Marianne Allure is experimental oil, acrylics and watercolor artist.

In her work she plays with landscape motives and her speciality is abstract painting.
Marianne studied Fine Art in Milan, Italy.

Inspiration

Marianne uses many diffrent mediums such as oils, acrylics, watercolors, pastels. She believes working with different mediums is bounderless. Each idea manifests in its own individual expressive style. She experiments with oil painting on canvas, acrylics and watercolors. In her work she plays with landscape motives and her speciality are abstracts. Also installations and design are not strangers to her. When it comes to art in which Marianne is truly in love with, she explores various techniques and she puts a lot of focus on constant exploration and learning which gives her more freedom to discover the wonderful cultural and creative world of art.

Marianne is an author of two books. She enjoys traveling which gives her opportunity to explore the world and get inspiration for her art work.

Marianne Allure Professional artist based in Europe. Studied Fine Art in Milan, Italy. Bachelor, Master Degree, Doctorate Student, B.F.A.
